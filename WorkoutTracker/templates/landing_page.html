User friendly

Our app features a user-friendly interface that makes tracking your workouts a breeze. You can easily create workout routines and customize them to your liking. You can also log the sets, reps, and weight lifted for each exercise.

Ready to use

You can use one of our built-in workout templates based on some of the most popular plans in the fitness world like Full Body Workout, Push/Pull/Legs or classic 3-day Split. Build your own templates with over 50 exercises form the Exercise Atlas.

Advanced Analytics

The app also provides detailed analytics and progress reports, allowing you to see how far you've come and identify areas for improvement. You can also set goals for yourself and track your progress towards achieving them.
